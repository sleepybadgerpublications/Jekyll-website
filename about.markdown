### About Sleepy Badger Productions

Sleepy Badger Productions was founded in 2024 in Northeast Florida to support small print runs for art books featuring local artists as well as organizing gallery openings and other promotional events.

For questions about publishing through Sleepy Badger Productions, please contact dan@sleepybadger.com. Unsolicited submissions will be destroyed without review.
